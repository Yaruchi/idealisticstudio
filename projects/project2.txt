PROJECT 02
Studio Website (Beta)
studio-Website.png

The Studio Website (Beta) is a portfolio website independently developed by one of the community members. Wanting to contribute something meaningful to the studio, Yaruchii—while not a member of the studio staff—designed and built the website as a personal initiative. What began as a project born from spare time evolved into a dedicated showcase of the studio's work, providing a central place to present its projects, creativity, and identity.
